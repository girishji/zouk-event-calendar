About

This app searches Facebook graph for Brazilian Zouk dance related events around the world. It is useful for travelers to get real time information about local Zouk events. You can sort results by location, popularity and time (default). This app also provides analytics for event organizers.

Facebook search is quirky. Results of the search depend on your social graph. However effort has been made to extract all possible information from Facebook graph. Sometimes you will see bogus events, often related to Caribbean Zouk. Many of these events are already removed by analyzing the attendance pattern of popular Zouk events, but a few slip through.

If you discover Zouk events that are not listed here, or if you find bugs, please report them.
