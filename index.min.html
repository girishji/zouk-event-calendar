About

This app searches Facebook graph for Brazilian Zouk dance events around the world. It is useful for travelers to get real time information about Zouk events happening in any place. You can sort results by location, popularity and start time (default). On the top right corner, this app displays the total number of available events, and a count of unique profiles (individuals) who are attending at least one of the well known festivals (listed in the link). This gives a measure of the 'scene'.

Effort has been made to filter out events related to Caribbean Zouk based on analyzing the attendance pattern of relevant events, but this process is not fool proof. All the filtered events are displayed in a separate tab.

Finally, since this is a Facebook app you are required to login to the app in addition to Facebook. However, this app does not have access to your personal information.
